CRISPY

Description

A delicious, cost-effective recipe for crispy baked chicken strips. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • Nonstick cooking spray
  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 cups plain dry bread crumbs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with foil; spray with cooking spray.
  2. Cut chicken breasts into thin strips, about 1-inch wide.
  3. Mix bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, parsley, black pepper, and salt in a shallow dish.
  4. Dip chicken strips in beaten eggs, then coat with bread crumb mixture.
  5. Place coated chicken strips on the prepared baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until crispy and golden brown, about 20 minutes.
